# True Artificial Life: Project "New World" Manifesto

## 1. Core Philosophy
The ultimate goal is to look at the screen and feel, with absolute certainty, that what we are seeing is *alive*. 

## 2. The Rejection of "Game Design" Biases
We must relentlessly eliminate any "God-like" programming. Life must invent its own biology from the raw physical laws of the universe.

## 3. The Path Chosen: The Math Void
We abandon Earth physics (Gravity, Space, RigidBodies). We embrace the Math Void. 
The universe is a massive 2D array of raw floating-point numbers (`0.0` to `1.0`). There are no entities. There is only the mathematical ripple of the numbers.

## 4. The Final Rejection: No Hardcoded Rules
We do not even hardcode the mathematical equation (the "Law of Physics") that updates the grid. If we choose a Gaussian bell curve, we are playing God by forcing our math onto the void. 

## 5. The Evolution of Physics (The Ultimate Strategy)
To achieve true, unguided emergence, we must evolve the laws of physics themselves.
1. **Generate the Law:** The computer randomly generates a mathematical equation (a "Math Tree" combining random operators like `+`, `*`, `Sin`, `Log`, `Mod`) to act as the Physics Rule.
2. **Run the Universe:** We apply this random equation to the grid. 
3. **The Filter:** 99.9% of random equations will result in instant death (the screen turns pure black or pure white) or pure static (no stable structures). 
4. **The Search:** If the universe dies to static, the computer throws that physics rule away, generates a new random equation, and tries again. 
5. **The Emergence:** We let the computer cycle through thousands of random universes until it hits an equation where a self-sustaining, moving pattern forms in the grid. 

## 6. Conclusion
We are not evolving creatures. We are evolving the fundamental laws of a mathematical universe, searching blindly for the exact random equation that naturally breeds structure. When we find it, whatever crawls out of that math is truly, undeniably *new*.
